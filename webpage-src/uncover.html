Comet





Comets are chunks of rocks and ice that orbit the sun. That's right, comets are extremely COLD, not hot! It's comprised of the nucelus (the solid, central part of the comet) and 2 trails (gas trail and dust trail). Fun fact, scientists found amino acids in samples taken from a comet, which are the building blocks of life! This suggests that comets helped bring life to Earth.
